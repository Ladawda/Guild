## Email Tips

### Subject Line Best Practices
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use their name when possible
- Be specific about content
- Avoid spam words: "FREE", "URGENT", "ACT NOW"

### Timing
- Welcome emails: Immediate
- Intros: Within 4 hours of match
- Follow-ups: Exactly 7 days after intro
- Digests: Weekly, same day/time

### Personalization Variables
Always personalize with:
- [First Name]
- [City/Location]
- [Quest Type]
- [Company Name] (for adventurers)

### Tracking
Add UTM parameters to links:
```
?utm_source=email&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=welcome_adventurer
```
