# Contractie Design Vision

## Brand Personality

Contractie is the friendly neighbor who knows all the best contractors. Our brand personality is defined by five core adjectives:

1. **Trustworthy** — We verify every contractor, show real reviews, and never hide fees. Our design communicates reliability through clear information hierarchy and honest presentation.

2. **Local** — We're rooted in neighborhoods. Our design emphasizes community connections, local knowledge, and the feeling of "someone nearby who can help."

3. **Approachable** — No corporate stiffness. We speak human, use warm visuals, and make the intimidating process of home improvement feel manageable.

4. **Capable** — We know our stuff. The interface feels professional and organized, giving users confidence that they'll find the right person for the job.

5. **Helpful** — Every element serves a purpose. We anticipate needs, remove friction, and guide users toward successful outcomes.

---

## Design Principles

### 1. Map-First Discovery
The map isn't a feature—it's the foundation. Every design decision prioritizes spatial context. Users should always know *where* contractors are located relative to *their* location.

### 2. Trust Through Transparency
Show, don't tell. Display verification badges prominently, expose review counts, reveal pricing ranges upfront. Hidden information erodes trust.

### 3. Progressive Disclosure
Don't overwhelm. Start simple (trade + location), then layer in filters, details, and options as users engage. The interface should feel lighter than competitors.

### 4. Community Over Corporation
Avoid sterile corporate aesthetics. Use real photography, neighborhood names, local landmarks. The product should feel like it belongs to the community it serves.

### 5. Action-Oriented Design
Every screen should have a clear primary action. Reduce decision fatigue through smart defaults and guided flows. Get users to their goal in the fewest steps possible.

## User Emotions to Evoke

### For Homeowners
- **Relief** — "Finally, I can find someone reliable without calling 10 different people"
- **Confidence** — "I can see exactly who's nearby and what others think of them"
- **Control** — "I'm in charge of this process, not at the mercy of contractors"
- **Excitement** — "My project is actually going to happen"

### For Contractors
- **Opportunity** — "There's real work here that fits my skills"
- **Pride** — "My work and reputation are displayed professionally"
- **Fairness** — "This platform treats me like a partner, not a commodity"
- **Growth** — "I can build my business here"

## Key Differentiators from Competitors

### vs. Angi (formerly Angie's List)
| Angi | Contractie |
|------|------------|
| Corporate, subscription-focused | Local, transparent, no paywalls |
| List-heavy, directory feel | Map-centric, visual discovery |
| Hidden fees, lead buying | Clear pricing, direct connection |
| Generic national presence | Hyper-local neighborhood focus |

### vs. Thumbtack
| Thumbtack | Contractie |
|-----------|------------|
| Auction-style, contractors bid | Discovery-based, homeowners choose |
| Transaction-focused | Relationship-focused |
| Overwhelming quote process | Simple, direct contact |
| One-size-fits-all categories | Trade-specific workflows |

### Our Unique Position
1. **Visual Discovery** — Map-first beats list-first for local services
2. **No Middleman** — Direct contractor contact, no lead fees
3. **Neighborhood Context** — See who's actually nearby, not just "in your city"
4. **Trade Specialization** — Custom experiences for different contractor types
5. **Community Feel** — Reviews from your actual neighbors

## Design Philosophy Summary

> Contractie bridges the gap between the cold efficiency of corporate platforms and the chaos of word-of-mouth recommendations. We're building a tool that feels like asking your neighbor for advice—if your neighbor knew every contractor in town and had verified their work.

This philosophy guides every design decision: **local first, trust always, simplicity above all.**
