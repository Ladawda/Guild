## My Top Picks for "Bringing Everyone Together"

### #1: **Crew** (crew.work)
- Everyone's on the same crew
- Friendly, not corporate
- "Join the crew" = inclusive
- Works for both clients and contractors

### #2: **Gather** (gather.work)
- Literally brings people together
- Warm, inviting
- "Gather here for work"
- Community-focused

### #3: **Communo** (communo.io)
- Community + connection
- Sounds friendly, international
- Unique, brandable
- Implies togetherness

### #4: **Venn** (venn.work)
- Two circles overlapping = client + contractor
- Smart, mathematical
- Visual logo potential
- "Where we meet"

### #5: **Guild** (guild.work)
- Historical trade associations
- Professional, skilled
- "Join the guild"
- Premium feel

### #6: **Together** (together.work)
- Dead simple
- Inclusive message
- Hard to forget
- Says exactly what you do
