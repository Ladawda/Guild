# Guild - Domain & Branding

## The Concept

Anime guilds = job boards for adventurers
- **Quest board** = Job postings
- **Adventurers** = Contractors  
- **Guild hall** = Platform
- **Party formation** = Client + contractor match

Perfect metaphor for Contractie.

## Branding Angles

### Angle 1: Direct Anime Reference (Playful)
**Taglines:**
- "Post your quest"
- "Find your party"
- "Join the guild"
- "Every job is a quest"

**Visual:**
- Stylized guild hall icon
- Quest board aesthetic
- Class icons for trades (plumber = water class, electrician = lightning class)

**Voice:**
- "Ready to embark on your next project?"
- "Form your party"
- "Quest complete!"

### Angle 2: Professional Guild (Historical)
**Taglines:**
- "Masters of the trade"
- "Join the guild"
- "Where craftsmen gather"
- "The guild standard"

**Visual:**
- Medieval guild hall
- Tools as heraldry
- Traditional, established feel

**Voice:**
- "Master craftsmen, united"
- "The guild welcomes you"
- "By guild standards"

### Angle 3: Modern Community (Balanced)
**Taglines:**
- "Better together"
- "Your guild, your work"
- "Where pros gather"
- "Join the guild"

**Visual:**
- Modern geometric logo
- Abstract guild hall
- Clean, professional

## My Top Recommendations

### #1: **joinguild.com**
- "Join the guild" = perfect onboarding
- Anime vibe without being childish
- Memorable, actionable

### #2: **guild.pro**
- Short, professional
- Trade-focused TLD
- Clean, modern

### #3: **theguild.co**
- Simple, elegant
- "The" implies it's THE guild
- .co is modern alternative to .com

### #4: **getguild.com**
- Classic SaaS pattern
- Action-oriented
- Easy to remember

### #5: **guild.work**
- Clear what it does
- Available (probably)
- Functional

**UI Elements:**

## Comparison: Crew vs Guild

| | Crew | Guild |
|---|------|-------|
| **Vibe** | Team, friendly | Community, skilled |
| **Anime ref** | Weak | Strong |
| **Professional** | Casual | Established |
| **Historical** | Modern | Medieval guilds |
| **Onboarding** | "Join the crew" | "Join the guild" |
| **Unique** | Common word | More distinctive |

---

## My Pick: **Guild** with **joinguild.com** or **guild.pro**

The anime connection makes it memorable and gives you built-in metaphors:
- Quest = Job
- Adventurer = Contractor  
- Guild hall = Platform
- Party = Match

It's distinctive, professional, and has personality.

Check **joinguild.com** first — it's probably available and perfect.
