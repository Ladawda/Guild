## Value Proposition

### For Quest Givers
- **Launch**: FREE quest posting
- **Regular**: $10 flat fee (vs $50-100/lead elsewhere)
- Direct access to verified adventurers
- No middleman markup
- You choose your party

### For Adventurers
- **Founding**: $25/month unlimited (50% off forever)
- **Regular**: $49/month unlimited
- No per-lead fees
- No commission on quests completed
- Keep 100% of earnings
- Direct relationship with quest givers
